About me

My name is Ahmed Al-Jabri, I’m a recent BSc graduate from Aston University for Cybersecurity. My main areas of interest are Computer Systems and Networking, Distributed Systems and Secure Programming.

The title Thoughful Voyage mirrors the purpose of this blog which is to share reflective inquries, deep insights and analysis on various topics of interest that I come across in my learning journey.
