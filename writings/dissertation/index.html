Dissertation

Title: Investigating the effectiveness of major Internet protocols in addressing DNS privacy concerns.

In this paper I investigate a recent and ongoing problem of DNS privacy. The paper covers in depth analysis of the current issue with DNS privacy, why it is a problem and what the community has proposed to address this issue. As the investigation progresses I explain the underlying technologies that the proposed solutions contain and critically analyse and compare their performances using established machine learning methods.

Note: This paper was initially submitted as my dissertation project at university however, I intend to continue working on this investigation and so this document will continue to be developed as time goes by.
